Book Name: to kill mocking bird.
Author's Name: Harper Lee.
Book Summary:
Set in the racially charged American South during the 1930s,
the novel explores themes of racial injustice, moral growth, and compassion through the eyes of Scout Finch,
a young girl whose father defends an innocent black man accused of raping a white woman.
