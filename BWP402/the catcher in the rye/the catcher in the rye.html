Book Name: The Catcher In The Rye.
Author's Name: J.D. Salinger.
Book Summary:
Narrated by the teenage Holden Caulfield, the novel provides a poignant portrayal of adolescence and alienation.
Holden recounts his experiences in New York City,
grappling with themes of identity, loss, and the phoniness of adult society.
